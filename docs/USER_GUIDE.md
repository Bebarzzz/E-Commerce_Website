## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Click the **Login/Signup** button in the navigation bar
2. Select the **Sign Up** tab
3. Fill in your information:
   - **Username**: Choose a unique username
   - **Email**: Enter a valid email address
   - **Password**: Create a strong password with:
     - At least 8 characters
     - One uppercase letter
     - One lowercase letter
     - One number
     - One special character (!, @, #, $, etc.)
4. Click **Continue** to create your account
5. You'll be automatically logged in

### Logging In

1. Click **Login/Signup** in the navigation bar
2. Select the **Login** tab
3. Enter your **email** and **password**
4. Click **Continue**

## Using the Search Feature

### Quick Search

1. Locate the **search bar** at the top of any shop page
2. Type your search query (e.g., "Toyota", "SUV", "Automatic")
3. Results update automatically as you type
4. The search looks through:
   - Brand names
   - Model names
   - Car types (Sedan, SUV, Truck, etc.)
   - Engine specifications
   - Transmission types
   - Colors

### Advanced Filtering

Use the **filter options** to narrow down results:
- **Price Range**: Set minimum and maximum price
- **Condition**: Choose "New" or "Used"
- **Year**: Filter by manufacturing year
- **Mileage**: Set mileage range for used cars

### Search Tips

- Use specific terms for better results (e.g., "2023 Toyota Camry")
- Try searching by features (e.g., "Hybrid", "AWD", "4-Cylinder")
- Search is case-insensitive
- Partial matches are supported (e.g., "Toy" will find "Toyota")

## FAQs

### General Questions

**Q: Do I need an account to browse cars?**
A: No, you can browse our inventory without an account. However, you'll need to create an account or provide information during checkout to place an order.

**Q: Can I save cars to view later?**
A: Currently, cars added to your cart are saved to your session. We recommend noting down the car details or IDs of vehicles you're interested in.

**Q: Are prices negotiable?**
A: Please contact our support team to discuss pricing for specific vehicles.

### Shopping & Orders

**Q: Can I modify my order after placing it?**
A: Please contact support immediately at support@example.com if you need to modify an order.

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: Payment method details are collected during checkout. Our team will contact you to arrange payment and finalize the transaction.

**Q: Do you offer financing?**
A: Please contact our sales team to discuss financing options.

**Q: Can I view my order history?**
A: Order history features are currently being developed. You'll receive email confirmations for all orders.

### Delivery & Shipping

**Q: How long does delivery take?**
A: Delivery times vary based on your location. Our team will contact you with an estimated timeline after you place your order.

**Q: Do you ship internationally?**
A: Please contact our support team to inquire about international shipping.

**Q: Can I pick up the car in person?**
A: Yes! Contact us to arrange an in-person pickup or test drive.

### Technical Issues

**Q: The website is running slowly. What should I do?**
A: Try refreshing your browser or clearing your cache. If issues persist, contact support.

**Q: Images aren't loading. How do I fix this?**
A: Check your internet connection and try refreshing the page. Ensure JavaScript is enabled in your browser.

**Q: The chatbot isn't responding. What's wrong?**
A: The chatbot requires an active internet connection. Try refreshing the page. If the issue continues, contact support.

**Q: I forgot my password. How do I reset it?**
A: Password reset functionality is currently being developed. Please contact support@example.com for assistance.
